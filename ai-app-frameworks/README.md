# AI App Frameworks

Frameworks and app layers for shipping AI features, chat products, and agentic user experiences.

## Top tools

## Vercel AI SDK
- Link: https://github.com/vercel/ai
- Category: AI App Frameworks
- What it is: TypeScript toolkit for building AI-powered apps and agent experiences.
- Why it matters: Strong developer ergonomics for production web AI interfaces.
- Best for: Full-stack teams shipping AI features quickly.
- Best paired with: Next.js, LiteLLM, Langfuse.

## Chainlit
- Link: https://github.com/Chainlit/chainlit
- Category: AI App Frameworks
- What it is: Framework for building conversational AI interfaces and workflows.
- Why it matters: Fast path from backend logic to usable AI UI.
- Best for: Teams prototyping chat/assistant products.
- Best paired with: LangChain, LlamaIndex, Promptfoo.

## Flowise
- Link: https://github.com/FlowiseAI/Flowise
- Category: AI App Frameworks
- What it is: Visual builder for LLM workflows and agent-like automations.
- Why it matters: Low-code acceleration for AI workflow assembly.
- Best for: Semi-technical builders and rapid experimentation.
- Best paired with: LangChain, vector DBs.

## Dify
- Link: https://github.com/langgenius/dify
- Category: AI App Frameworks
- What it is: Open-source platform for building and operating LLM applications.
- Why it matters: Combines app building, deployment, and operational controls.
- Best for: Teams needing an integrated app platform.
- Best paired with: LiteLLM, observability tools.

## Honorable mentions

## Gradio
- Link: https://github.com/gradio-app/gradio
- Category: AI App Frameworks
- What it is: Python framework for building interactive ML/AI demos.
- Why it matters: Extremely fast prototyping for model and workflow interfaces.
- Best for: ML teams and demo-driven iteration.
- Best paired with: Local runtimes, evaluation flows.

## Streamlit
- Link: https://github.com/streamlit/streamlit
- Category: AI App Frameworks
- What it is: Data app framework widely used for AI prototypes and internal tools.
- Why it matters: Productizes AI demos into shareable web interfaces quickly.
- Best for: Teams shipping internal AI tools.
- Best paired with: RAG stacks and local inference.

## How to choose

- Use `Vercel AI SDK` for TypeScript-first product teams.
- Use `Chainlit` for conversational prototypes.
- Use `Flowise`/`Dify` when low-code orchestration speed matters.
